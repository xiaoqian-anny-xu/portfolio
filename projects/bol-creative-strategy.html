Bol: Het Grote Speelgoedboek

Creative strategy for Bol's Sinterklaas toys book

Project Overview

Our team developed a creative strategy to enhance Bol's Sinterklaas toys book, making it more inclusive and enjoyable for children from diverse backgrounds. We presented our insights and strategy to Bol, receiving positive feedback on our innovative approach to Het Grote Speelgoedboek.

The challenge was to reimagine a traditional Dutch holiday catalog in a way that resonates with the modern, diverse population of the Netherlands. Our approach focused on creating an interactive and inclusive experience that would appeal to families from all cultural backgrounds.

Key Strategies

Our strategy focused on creating an interactive and inclusive experience for children, incorporating elements such as augmented reality and personalized content. We also emphasized the importance of representing diverse families and cultures in the toy selection and presentation.

Some of the key elements of our strategy included:

  • Augmented reality features to bring toys to life
  • Personalized recommendations based on individual preferences
  • Diverse representation in product selection and imagery
  • Interactive storytelling elements to engage children
  • Sustainability focus to appeal to environmentally conscious families
