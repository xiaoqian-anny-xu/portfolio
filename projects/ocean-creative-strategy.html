OceanOutdoor Creative Strategy

Capturing the essence of being an "Amsterdammer" for digital screens

Keywords: creative strategy, presentation deck, user research, content creation, ideation, visual storytelling, Canva design, customer insights, cultural representation, team collaboration

I attended the Plusplusone creative workshop, where I worked on real-life cases for Bol and Ocean Outdoor. Collaborating with my teammates, we developed creative strategies and ideations, which we presented to the clients. I took the lead in creating and finetuning the presentation decks and thoroughly enjoyed visualizing our ideas. In the end, I stepped into the role of director to produce a video based on our insights.

Project Challenge

Ocean Outdoor, a company specializing in digital billboards and advertising screens, challenged us to identify a subculture in Amsterdam and visually capture their feelings and definition of what it means to be an "Amsterdammer" on digital screens.

Our team selected the Heesterveld creative community subculture and developed a strategy to showcase their unique culture—highlighting the home-like environment that fosters active creativity. We then crafted a creative idea based on this strategy.

Our Approach - Strategy

Strategy presentation deck

My team (Charen, Stanley, Devesh, and I) explored the Heesterveld creative community, a vibrant and artistic neighbourhood in Bijlmer, Amsterdam. I conducted an interview along with Charen, repeatedly analyzed the transcripts, and reviewed social media content to uncover key insights about this community. We discovered that a sense of home and mutual support is crucial for artists to thrive.

Therefore, our strategy focused on recreating the sense of home and belonging that once defined this community, guiding our approach to strategy and ideation.

Our Approach - Ideation

Ideation presentation deck

After presenting our strategy to the client and receiving feedback, we continued to turn the strategy into actionable ideas. Through brainstorming, we developed the concept of a video that showcases how sweet and warm the creative community is:

A typical Amsterdammer encountered an untypical Amsterdammer, and followed her lead into the world of another side of Amsterdam - Heesterveld. He witnesses all kinds of possibilities happening in the community and is warmly welcomed to create and have fun together. In the end, he has a good time and feels at home in this community.

Our approach was selected by Ocean Outdoor and was later produced as a short video.

Presentation decks

I led the creation of both the strategy and ideation decks. To ensure a cohesive look that aligned with Ocean Outdoor’s brand, I started by selecting a clean and simple Canva template that reflected their style. I then customized the template by finding images and design elements that fit our content, modifying the overall style to communicate our ideas.
